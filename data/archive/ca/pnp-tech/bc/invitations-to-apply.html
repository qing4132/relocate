Invitations to apply

Last updated: May 15, 2026 

Periodically, the BC PNP invites candidates in its registration pool to apply to the program. Candidates that are deemed most likely to meet B.C.’s economic and labour market needs are selected based on their registration information. 

Invitations to apply (ITA) are issued to both Skills Immigration and Entrepreneur Immigration registrants. To find out if you received an ITA, please log into your BC PNP Online User Profile.

April 22, 2026

On April 22, 2026, the BC PNP issued invitations to apply to 484 candidates who will create high economic impact in B.C. Invitations were issued to candidates with:  

  • A minimum wage of $62/hour and $125,000/year, and a job offer in NOC TEER 0, 1, 2 or 3 (252 candidates), or
  • A minimum score of 138 points (232 candidates). 

Future high economic impact invitations to apply may be based on different factors. Please refer to section 7.3(a) of the Skills Immigration Program Guide.

Please note: Invitations to apply are issued to qualified registrations received before the date of the draw. 

February 11, 2026

On February 11, 2026, the BC PNP issued invitations to apply to 460 candidates who will create high economic impact in B.C. Invitations were issued to candidates with:  

  • A minimum wage of $62/hour and $125,000/year, and a job offer in NOC TEER 0, 1, 2 or 3 (195 candidates), or
  • A minimum score of 135 points (265 candidates). 

Future high economic impact invitations to apply may be based on different factors. Please refer to section 7.3(a) of the Skills Immigration Program Guide.

Please note: Invitations to apply are issued to qualified registrations received before the date of the draw. 

February 4, 2026

On February 4, 2026, the BC PNP issued invitations to apply to 429 candidates who will create high economic impact in B.C. Invitations were issued to candidates with:  

  • A minimum wage of $70/hour and $145,000/year, and a job offer in NOC TEER 0, 1, 2 or 3 (206 candidates), or
  • A minimum score of 138 points (223 candidates). 

Future high economic impact invitations to apply may be based on different factors. Please refer to section 7.3(a) of the Skills Immigration Program Guide.

Please note: Invitations to apply are issued to qualified registrations received before the date of the draw. 

To see previous invitations to apply, please visit the Archives page.
